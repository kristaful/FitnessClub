Class Descriptions

Cross Training
A great way to give your workouts variety! A fun mix of strength training, HIIT, and barre techniques.
Sprint
Fast and Seriously Fun! Squeeze in 30 minutes of this aerobic class and get energized for your day.
Spin
Bring your water bottles and a thick towel, you're gonna sweat it out in this spin class. Great tunes and the latest spin bikes. Sign up now, these seats go quick!
BodyPump
High energy, lots of fun! This class is designed to get your heart pumping and your body on the move with a balanced mix of strength training and aerobics.
BodyCombat
Get ready to burn it up! Military style training with a heavy additon of music and spirit.
Step Interval
Not your average step class. Join us for a blend of step aerobics and dance training.
Advanced Cross Training
For those who want to take it to the next level. This advanced class mixes various forms of exercises, which change on a monthly basis, designed for athletes.
MixxedFIT
A high intensity ciruict style strength and aerobic blend for all.
Zumba
Get in here and dance! Our certified Zumba instructor knows how to get you grooving to the music. This class is designed for everyone.
